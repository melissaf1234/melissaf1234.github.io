About / Sobre

Melissa Florence Marques Miécimo da Silva is a biomedical scientist with training in biochemistry from Universidade Estácio de Sá and specialization in Immunology. Her experience includes neurobiology of behavior, neurophysiology, clinical-analysis routines, biosafety, virology, and technical participation in clinical and laboratory research at UFRJ.

Research and professional interests

  • Immunology and applied human health
  • Virology and vaccine-development workflows
  • Nanobiosystems and mRNA vaccine candidates
  • Neurobiology, neurophysiology, and behavioral disorders
  • Clinical analysis, biosafety, and laboratory practice
  • Real-world data, epidemiology, and public-health training

Education / Formação

  • Specialization in Immunology, Faculdade Metropolitana de São Paulo, 2021-2022. Final work: Imunologia Aplicada à Saúde Humana.
  • Graduate research in Nanobiosystems, UFRJ, 2022-2025. Project: in vitro evaluation of an mRNA-technology yellow fever vaccine candidate.
  • BSc in Biomedicine, Universidade Estácio de Sá, 2018-2021. Thesis: nanoparticles for site-directed drug delivery in neurodegenerative diseases, with emphasis on Alzheimer's disease.
  • Technical training, FAETEC, 2014.

Research experience / Experiência em pesquisa

  • FAPERJ scholarship holder at UFRJ, 2022-2024, with exclusive dedication.
  • FAPERJ TCT2 scholarship holder, 2017-2018, working on biochemical aspects related to susceptibility to Alzheimer's disease caused by neonatal infection.
  • Scientific initiation at UFRJ, 2016-2018, with work connected to physiology, biochemistry, clinical analysis, cellular immunology, biophysics, and neuroscience.
